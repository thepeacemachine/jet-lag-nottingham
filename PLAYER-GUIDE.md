### Your Defense:

**Drawing Cards:**
- You draw after EVERY question you answer
- Maximum 6 cards in hand
- Discard extras if needed

**Card Types You Get:**
- **Veto** - Cancel a question (they lose it!)

- **Duplicate** - Copy a card you have

### Strategy Tips:
- Save Vetos for important questions (RADAR, PHOTO)
- Collect Time cards for higher score
- Keep hand space for good cards
- Don't waste Veto on early questions

### Your Score:
